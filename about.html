Hello!

My name is Emily Wu and I am an event planner from New York City. Ever since being a part of the student government in my high school, I have accrued an interest in planning events. An event planning internship during my undergraduate studies only furthered my excitement to work in the events industry.

After graduating from NYU with a masters degree in event management, I am ready to bring my passion to producing fun and memorable events. I hope to be able to use events to engage the community and create a positive impact on people and society.
