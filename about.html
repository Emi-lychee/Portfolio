Hello!

My name is Emily Wu and I am a multimedia artist from New York City. I have a passion for creating because I like sharing my ideas, experiencing how others react to my work, and producing a product I can take pride in.

As a Hunter College student and Brooklyn Technical High School alum, I have six years of experience in creating different types of multimedia. During this time, I developed an expertise in using Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Premiere Pro, as well as HTML and CSS. With these skills and more, I aspire to have a positive impact on society by becoming a distinguished artist that can spread happiness with my artwork and designs.
